Coffee Grinder Model

This model is the first model I decided to create after finishing the blender chair tutorial. It is based off of a wooden manual coffee bean grinder that I have had for a number of years. The reason I chose to replicate this was because of the interesting curves and shapes (the handle, knob, and the dome on top) gave me a challenge and allowed me to utilise the skills learnt on the chair.

The rendered version has dynamic materials created in blender for imperfections like metallic smudges and bumps; these aren't exported in the real-time model, as GLTF does not support these out-of-the-box.
